About Us

Welcome to Castomate Technologies

Your Partner in Comprehensive Foundry Solutions

At Castomate Technologies, we take immense pride in being a one-stop solution provider for the metal casting and foundry industry. Since 2007, we have been committed to delivering a diverse range of high-quality, cost-effective, and technically sound products tailored to meet the evolving needs of foundries across India and beyond.

Our Product Portfolio

We offer an extensive range of specialized products categorized into the following segments:

  • Induction Furnace Essentials
    Enhance your furnace efficiency with top-grade materials designed for optimal performance.

  • Melting Aids & Ferro Alloys
    Improve melting process efficiency using our high-purity alloys and additives.

  • Foundry Filters
    Prevent metallic intrusion and ensure defect-free castings with:

    • Silicon Carbide (SiC) Foam Filters
    • Zircon Foam Filters

  • Foundry Chemicals & Insulative Products
    Reliable supplies for:

    • Feeding system support
    • Shrinkage compensation
    • High-temperature stability
