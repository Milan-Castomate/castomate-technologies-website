About Us

Welcome to Castomate Technologies

Your Partner in Comprehensive Foundry Solutions

At Castomate Technologies, we take immense pride in being a one-stop solution provider for the metal casting and foundry industry. Since 2007, we have been committed to delivering a diverse range of high-quality, cost-effective, and technically sound products tailored to meet the evolving needs of foundries across India and beyond.

Our Extensive Product Portfolio

We offer an extensive range of specialized products categorized into the following segments:

  • Induction Furnace Essentials: Optimize your furnace performance with our top-notch products, designed for maximum efficiency and longevity.
  • Melting Aids and Ferro Alloys: Enhance the efficiency of your melting processes with our high-quality materials, formulated for superior metallurgical results.
  • Foundry Filters: Prevent metallic intrusions and ensure cleaner castings with our cutting-edge filters, including SiC Foam Filters and Zircon Foam Filters.
  • Foundry Chemicals and Insulative Products: From essential chemicals to advanced insulative sleeves that compensate for shrinkage, we provide everything required for flawless casting production.

Manufacturing Excellence & Quality Assurance

We don't just supply products; we manufacture them too! Our state-of-the-art manufacturing unit allows us to produce a wide array of critical foundry components, ensuring consistent quality and prompt availability to meet your specific needs. Our in-house manufactured products include:

  • Exothermic & Insulating Sleeves
  • APC & Thermaxo Powder
  • Ceramic Filters
  • Core Fix
  • Aluminum Paints / Coat
  • Foundry Chaplets
  • Cor Box Air Vents
  • Plastic Letters

This direct control over manufacturing means we can uphold the highest standards, ensuring reliability and performance in every product.

Comprehensive Import Offerings & Efficient Logistics

As key importers of crucial foundry products, we guarantee a diverse range of options to suit your specific requirements. Our imported product list includes:

  • Silicon Carbide Foam Filters
  • Zircon Foam Filters
  • Perlite Ore
  • Graphite Petroleum Coke (GPC / Artificial Graphite)
  • Steel Shots
  • Graphite Electrodes & many more...

With strategically located warehouses in Rajkot and Ahmedabad, we ensure efficient stock management and prompt fulfillment, especially for larger volume orders. This robust supply chain minimizes lead times and keeps your operations running smoothly.
